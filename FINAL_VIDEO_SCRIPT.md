## 🎬 Recording Tips

### Before Recording:

1. **Open the link:** https://careeradvisoraws.vercel.app
2. **Zoom browser:** Press Ctrl/Cmd + (zoom to 125-150%)
3. **Close other tabs:** Only demo tab open
4. **Test everything:** Click all buttons, type in search
5. **Practice once:** Do a full run-through
6. **Set timer:** 3 minutes countdown
7. **Start recording:** Take a deep breath!

### During Recording:

1. **Speak clearly** - Not too fast, not too slow
2. **Show enthusiasm** - You're excited about this!
3. **Point to screen** - Help viewers follow along
4. **Don't apologize** - Everything is working perfectly
5. **Check timer** - At 1:30, should be at form

### After Recording:

1. **Watch the video** - Check audio and video quality
2. **Verify timing** - Should be under 3 minutes
3. **Check all features shown** - Use checklist above
4. **Export in HD** - 1920x1080, 30fps, MP4 format
