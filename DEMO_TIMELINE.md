## 🎤 Speaking Pace Guide

**Too Fast:** ❌
- Judges can't follow
- Sounds nervous
- Miss important points

**Too Slow:** ❌
- Boring
- Won't finish in time
- Lose attention

**Just Right:** ✅
- Clear and confident
- Finish at 2:55-3:00
- Judges understand everything

**Tip:** Practice with a timer 3 times!
